About Me

Hello there. I'm Dan, an aspiring web developer and student based in Buffalo, New York. I am currently attending University at Buffalo fulltime and entering my second year in Fall 2016. My passion for computers and technology is complimented by a great attention to detail and creative mind; tailored perfectly for the web dev world.

I served six years active duty in the U.S. Navy and was honorably discharged in September 2015. This once in a lifetime experience allowed me see great places and meet great people along three at-sea/overseas deployments.

Outside of development I am an avid Game of Thrones follower (Team Stark!) and enjoy watching hockey, football, and MMA. I have recently acquired a dedication to weightlifting and have an extensive Steam library of games that will never be played.

I am expanding my skillset and striving to learn something new every day. I am always looking for new work, new projects, or new ways of doing things. Please feel free to contact me at the bottom of this page.
